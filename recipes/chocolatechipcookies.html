Chocolate Chip Cookies

Learn how to make them like your grandma used to

These chocolate chip cookies will come fresh out of the oven golden brown and perfectly crispy. This recipe has stood the test of time, and will continue to result in absolutely perfect cookies for years to come!

Make sure you allocate all of the ingredients before you start making them. You don't want to end up halfway through the baking process before you realize you're out of vanilla extract!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 34 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • (optional) 1 cup chopped nuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375° and line two large baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. In a large bowl, using a hand mixer, beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add flour and mix until just combined. Fold in chocolate chips and nuts (if using).
  4. Using a small cookie scoop, scoop cookie dough onto baking sheets at least 2” apart.
  5. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden.
