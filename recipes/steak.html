Steak

Bring the steakhouse to your kitchen table

Making the right steak is easy when you have proper directions. This modern steak recipe will have the family pets foaming at the mouth at the aroma of delicious steak.

In order to prepare for this recipe make sure you buy the best steak you can comfortably afford. Remember - cheaper doesn't always mean worse! With the right preparation any steak can be fantastic.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened and divided
  • 1 tsp. minced fresh parsley
  • 1/2 tsp. minced garlic
  • 1/4 tsp. reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 beef flat iron steak or boneless top sirloin steak (3/4 pound)
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper

Steps

  1. Mix 1 tablespoon butter with parsley, garlic and soy sauce.
  2. Sprinkle steak with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat remaining butter over medium heat. Add steak; cook until meat reaches desired doneness (for medium-rare, a thermometer should read 135°; medium, 140°; medium-well, 145°), 4-7 minutes per side. Serve with garlic butter.
